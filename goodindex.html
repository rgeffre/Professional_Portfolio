Design Process

The design process is key to the projects success. We'll make sure we understand your goals and requirements, reach agreement on design during each phase, and communicate frequently throughout the process to ensure your project meets all your needs.

  • 01. Research
  • 02. Design
  • 03. Development

We will work together to determine the goals and requirements for your project. I'll use everything we discovered and research the best options for meeting your needs, and then we'll choose the best solutions for the project. We'll create the project specifications and then move into the design phase.


Using the specifications we created during our research I will create a project plan and design for your site or application. This can include mockups and test apps to finalize design decisions. Once we have a final design we will move into the final phase.


Using the final design we'll update the project plan and begin development of the site or application.
